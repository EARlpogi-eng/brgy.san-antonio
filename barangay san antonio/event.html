Barangay Assembly of San Antonio

The Barangay Assembly of San Antonio is a local meeting where residents discuss community issues... It includes updates on projects, financial reports, and public safety. Led by the barangay captain and council, it allows residents to voice concerns and collaborate on community improvements, promoting transparency and participatory governance.

4Ps Cleaning Association

The "4Ps Cleaning Association" is a group of Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program beneficiaries dedicated to keeping their community clean and orderly... They organize regular clean-up drives, educate residents on proper waste management, and promote health and environmental awareness, fostering community spirit and improving the local environment.
